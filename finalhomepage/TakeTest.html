Cognitive Test

Instructions for Attending the Test

Welcome to the cognitive test. Please read the instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • Ensure you are in a quiet environment to concentrate on the test.
  • The test consists of three sections: Empathy, Adaptability, and Communication.
  • Each section has 5 questions, and each question is rated on a scale from 1 to 5.
  • Once you complete a section, you cannot go back and change your answers.
  • Your final score will be calculated based on your responses across all sections.

Good luck!

Scoring Methodology

Each question is to be rated on a scale from 1 to 5:

  • 1 = Strongly Disagree
  • 2 = Disagree
  • 3 = Neutral
  • 4 = Agree
  • 5 = Strongly Agree

Your score for each section is the sum of your responses.
